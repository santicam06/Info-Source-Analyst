✅ **CORROBORATED – Performance Improvements:**
- o1 achieved 83.3% on AIME 2024 (vs. GPT-4's 9%)
- o1 achieved 1,673 Elo on CodeForces (vs. GPT-4's 808)
- o3 achieved 87.5% on ARC-AGI benchmark (human performance ~85%)
- These benchmarks are recognized standards in AI research

✅ **CORROBORATED – "Thinking Harder" Metaphor:**
- Multiple sources describe test-time scaling using this language
- Models do allocate more computation during inference (test time)
- OpenAI o1 uses internal "reasoning tokens" (hidden from user) for extended thinking

✅ **CORROBORATED – Emerging Paradigm:**
- Widely discussed in AI research community
- Multiple companies implementing similar approaches (Google Gemini, DeepSeek, Anthropic)
- Represents genuine shift from traditional scaling approaches
